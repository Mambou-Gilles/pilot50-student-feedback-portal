Frequently Asked Questions

When should I submit my self evaluations?

Self evaluation should be submitted every Friday by the end of the day.

What if I submit late due to an unforeseen situation?

Late submission comes with a penalty: you will receive the lowest grade, placing you in the list of average performers.

What happens if I forget to submit?

You will receive a time deduction of 19 hours if you attended the soft skills meeting, or 20 hours otherwise along with a Course Correction Plan (CCP).

What if I only book 1 English Chat Session instead of 2?

Communicate with your Team Lead immediately. Lack of communication results in being placed on the average performer list.
